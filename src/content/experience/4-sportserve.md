Sportserve offers sports-booking and sports-betting solutions to thousands of businesses around the world. These businesses need to maintain a pulse on their market performance to stay profitable. We on the Business Inteligence Team work on ensuring key business insights remain available to our customers in near realtime. This involves aggreagating data from multiple systems, and running analysis on this data to discover insights. An unstated goal of our team is to ensure we do this without impairing the performance of the systems originating the data.
